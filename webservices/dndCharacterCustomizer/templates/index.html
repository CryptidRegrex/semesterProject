Welcome to Character Customizer

Create, randomize, and manage your Dungeons & Dragons 5e characters and campaigns.

Feature 1: Character Creation

Manage your Dungeons & Dragons 5e characters! Choose their race, class, abilities, and backstory to craft heroes or villains for your campaigns. Whether you're a seasoned player or a beginner, this tool offers a guided, user-friendly experience to bring your imagination to life.

Feature 2: Character Randomization

Try our character randomization feature! Generate entirely new characters with the click of a button. This feature ensures the characters are within the rules of 5e, making it perfect for quick games or discovering new combinations you might not have considered.

Feature 3: Campaign-Character Management

Seamlessly integrate your characters into campaigns. With our management tools, you can assign characters to specific campaigns for easy Dungeon Master and player tracking. Easily update a character's current status via the update management feature.
